Welcome

Hi, I’m Elle! I'm a licensed Doctor of Physical Therapy with certifications in holistic health, personal training, health coaching, ADHD coaching, and Reiki energy healing. I help people reconnect with their bodies, energy, and purpose through personalized care and grounded healing work.

With a deep respect for science and spirit, I guide clients in bridging the gap between conventional care and healing at the soul level. Whether you’re navigating life's ups and downs, managing chronic illness, or simply seeking more alignment, I’m here to support your transformation.

Healing Beach was born out of my desire to hold space for the ebb and flow of growth and restoration. This is my way of branching out to bring wellness to those who seek it across the globe. When I’m not working with clients, you’ll probably find me enjoying the ocean breeze, creating something with my hands, or caring for my turtle, dog, and many fish.

What I Offer

  • Health & Wellness Coaching
    Tailored support for healthy lifestyle changes, transforming you physically, emotionally, and mentallly. Health coaching includes methods for stress reduction and management, improving self-confidence and motivation, managing chronic illness and navigating the medical system, and improving overall fitness. I also have a certification in ADHD coaching, helping not only clients with ADHD, but also parents, spouses, employers, and educators of individuals with ADHD.
  • Reiki & Energy Healing
    I combine Reiki energy healing and frequency healing with tuning forks to clear energy blockages, promote calm, and restore inner balance.
  • Personal Training
    Individualized to meet your fitness goals, training includes creation of a customized workout plan, education on exercise techniques and proper body mechanics, guidance for variety and progression, and motivation.
  • Physical Therapy
    As a licensed physical therapist, I evaluate and treat a variety of conditions, including musculoskeletal injuries, neurological diseases, and chronic illness. I am certified in LSVT BIG, which is a specialized program to treat those with Parkinson's disease. I am licenced to practice physical therapy in South Carolina.
